Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BrewCrew Club, our community, and our mission.

What is BrewCrew Club?

BrewCrew Club is India's first coffee-only community platform. We connect coffee lovers with the best local cafés and reward them for their passion.

How do I earn rewards?

You can earn points by checking in at partner cafés, posting reviews, and making purchases through the app. These points can then be redeemed for free drinks, merchandise, and other exclusive perks.

How can I partner with BrewCrew Club?

We'd love to have you! You can get in touch with us through our "Message Us" section on the homepage, and our partnership team will reach out to you with all the details.
